The Walk

The walk from Monterosso to Riomaggiore will take approximately two hours, give or take an hour depending on the weather conditions and your phisical shape.

What?

Cinque Terre comprises five villages: Monterosso, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola and Riomaggiore

Where?

On the northwest cost of the Italian Riviera, north of the city La Spezia.

Price?

The walk is free!
