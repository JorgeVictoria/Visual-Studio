Lightness

With HSL colors, 0 % lightness means black, and 100 lightness means white.

hsl(0, 100%, 100%)

hsl(0, 100%, 80%)

hsl(0, 100%, 60%)

hsl(0, 100%, 40%)

hsl(0, 100%, 20%)

hsl(0, 100%, 0%)

Shades of grey

Shades of gray are often defined by setting the hue and saturation to 0, and adjust the lightness from 0% to 100% to get darker/lighter shades.

hsl(0, 0%, 20%)

hsl(0, 0%, 30%)

hsl(0, 0%, 40%)

hsl(0, 0%, 60%)

hsl(0, 0%, 70%)

hsl(0, 0%, 90%)

HSLA color

It's an extension of HSL with an Alpha Channel, who specifies the opacity for a color.

hsla(9, 100%, 64%, 0.2)

hsla(9, 100%, 64%, 0.4)

hsla(9, 100%, 64%, 0.6)
